State

My first year of joining FBLA I went to state in the year of 2018-2019. There I competed in E-business along with marketing. I presented my website that was based on a photography webiste called Golden Cove Photography. Me and my partner Abraham decided to do photography because we felt that a photography business was something that our community could have benefited from. We showed off the benefits of our website along with what we offer as to scheduling, prices and our content. We ending up placing second in the event.
